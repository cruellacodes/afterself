Why It Works

Built for the worst day of your life.

📱

No app needed

Works with any phone that can send a text. No internet connection, no wallet app, no QR codes. Just SMS.

🔒

Your keys stay yours

You sign the transaction on your own device during setup. The server stores only presigned bytes, never your private key.

✕

One code, one transfer

Each code works exactly once. After use, the nonce advances and the transaction can never be replayed.

⌚

Rate limited

One attempt every 60 seconds. Brute force is impractical. Every attempt, success or failure, is logged.

🌎

400,000 pickup locations

150+ countries. In the handful of places where WU/MoneyGram doesn't exist, we connect you with a local peer to peer contact for a direct cash meetup.

☑

Full audit trail

Every setup, verification, execution, and failure is logged with timestamps. Nothing happens silently.
